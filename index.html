Before You Begin

This experience follows a real family narrative.

As you move through the story, you will encounter original archival photographs.

  • Hover over an image to reveal a generated summary of what the system sees.
  • Click on an image to enter AI-generated interpretations based on that specific moment in the memory.

From there, you can continue clicking through additional generated images. These images are speculative visual expansions.

You may find yourself moving further away from the original narrative and into entirely AI-generated material.

To return to the main story and continue through the narrative, locate and click one of the original archival images.
