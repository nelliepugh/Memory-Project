Augmented Memories

ABOUT

Augmented Memories explores what happens when personal archives meet generative systems. At this intersection of personal memory and generative AI, this project aims to highlight the fragility of memory and its reconstructive process, exploring what happens when it is subjected to technological speculation. The generative images produced in this project are speculations on real lived memory. Whilst they appear convincing they are not true lived experience but probable extensions produced from archival imagery. The reader is asked to consider what is real and what is generated.
